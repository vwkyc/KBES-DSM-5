DSM-5-TR Mental Health Screening

This screening tool evaluates symptoms based on DSM-5-TR criteria for: Major Depressive Disorder (296.2x), Bipolar Disorder (296.4x), Generalized Anxiety Disorder (300.02), ADHD (314.0x), Schizophrenia (295.90), OCD (300.3), and PTSD (309.81).

Methodology

  • Questions weighted by symptom specificity and severity (None/Mild/Moderate/Severe)
  • 70% threshold of disorder-specific symptoms required
  • Certainty score based on symptom presence and severity
  • Dynamic question weights based on symptom specificity
